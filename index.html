A Letter for Jasm

My dearest Jasm,

As I sit here writing this on our first anniversary, I can't help but smile thinking about how far we've come. A whole year ago, I never could have imagined that someone could mean so much to me, especially someone I've never been able to hold in my arms.

But that's the thing about love, isn't it? It transcends physical boundaries. It lives in your voice during our late-night calls, in the way you make me laugh until my sides hurt, in those quiet moments when we just listen to each other breathe.

This year has taught me that distance is just a number, but connection is everything. We've created something beautiful together - our own little world of inside jokes, conversations that stretch for hours, shared dreams, and those perfect moments of silence that say more than words ever could.

I love how your voice is the first thing I want to hear in the morning and the last thing I need at night. I love how you remember every story I tell you, how you make ordinary days extraordinary just by being part of them. I love our traditions - the way we count down New Year's together from different time zones, how we plan what we'll cook for each other someday.

You've shown me patience during my worst moments, kindness when I didn't deserve it, and a love that doesn't need to be proven every day because it simply is. You've made me believe that some things are worth waiting for, worth fighting for, worth every sleepless night missing you.

I won't lie - this year has been hard. There were nights I cried because I couldn't hold you through your tough times, moments when the distance felt impossible, days when I questioned if we were crazy for trying. But every challenge has only proven that what we have is unbreakable.

Looking ahead, I see everything we've talked about becoming real. I see our first apartment together, lazy Sunday mornings in bed, cooking dinner side by side instead of over video calls. I see meeting your family, you meeting mine, building a life that started with two hearts that refused to let distance win.

Thank you for this incredible year, Jasm. Thank you for choosing us when it would have been easier to choose someone closer. Thank you for being my anchor, my home, my future.

Here's to closing the distance, to building our forever, and to proving that real love really can conquer anything. I love you more than words, more than miles, more than I ever thought possible.

All my love,
Always yours
